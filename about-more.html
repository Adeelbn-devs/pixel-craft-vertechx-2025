About VertechX & MVJCE

MVJ College of Engineering

Established in 1982, MVJ College of Engineering is a premier institution in Bangalore, dedicated to excellence in engineering education. [1] With a sprawling campus and state-of-the-art facilities, MVJCE has been a nurturing ground for tech talent for over four decades. The college is committed to developing ethically strong and skilled engineers who can meet the challenges of a globalized world. [1] It is an autonomous institution under Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). [1]

VertechX: The Legacy

VertechX is the flagship annual technical festival of MVJCE. It's a vibrant platform that brings together students from various colleges to showcase their technical prowess, compete in exciting events, and learn from industry experts. From coding challenges and hackathons to robotics competitions and paper presentations, VertechX offers a diverse range of activities. The fest aims to foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning among the student community, making it one of the most anticipated tech events in the region.
